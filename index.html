Departments

Indoor Patient Department

The hospital has a general ward, special rooms for managing indoor patients. Government trained staff for indoor care of patients post major surgeries.

Outdoor Patient Department

Doctors are available to consult 24/7. Patient can consult for any types of diseases and it is completely free of cost.

Ayush

AYUSH department is available to tackle life style disorder that is a threat to the society.

Operation Theater

Clean and spacious rooms with overhead surgical lights are available for all kinds of operations.
